# Dashboards

## 1. Medication Compliance Dashboard
- **Charts:** 
  - Bar chart of medications taken vs skipped per patient.
  - Pie chart of compliance percentage per department.
- **Filters:** Patient ID, Department, Date Range.
- **KPI Metrics:** Compliance percentage, total medications missed.

## 2. Audit Activity Dashboard
- **Charts:** 
  - Line chart of system actions over time.
  - Top 5 employees with most actions.
- **Filters:** Employee, Action Type, Date Range.
- **KPI Metrics:** Number of unauthorized attempts, average actions per user.

## 3. Inventory Dashboard
- **Charts:** 
  - Monthly inventory usage (line chart).
  - Stock level distribution (pie chart).
- **Filters:** Item, Department, Month.
- **KPI Metrics:** Low stock alerts, reorder frequency.
